Academic Programs

Basic Education

St. Peter's College provides a nurturing basic education program from Grade 1 to Grade 12, fostering academic excellence and character development. With dedicated faculty, interactive teaching methods, and modern resources, students engage in a holistic learning experience. The curriculum integrates core subjects with co-curricular activities, promoting critical thinking, creativity, and effective communication. Emphasizing values and community service, students develop leadership skills and social responsibility. Personalized attention ensures each student's growth, preparing them for higher education and a positive societal impact.

Tertiary Education

St. Peter's College offers a dynamic tertiary education experience, fostering intellectual growth and professional success. With renowned faculty and diverse programs, students acquire knowledge, skills, and practical experiences for the global workforce. Research opportunities and industry collaborations fuel innovation. Emphasizing holistic development, the institution promotes ethical values, cultural understanding, and community engagement. Through comprehensive support services and a vibrant student community, graduates emerge as impactful contributors to a changing world.

Graduate Studies

The Graduate Studies department of St. Peter’s College, Iligan City is operated by virtue of the Government Recognition No. 119, series of 1974 which was issued by the Department of Education in 1974. The Department offers only the Graduate Program leading to the degree of Master of Arts in Education. The courses in this program are being taught by four (4) full time and eighteen (18) part time qualified instructors who are experts in their respective fields. In addition, the Graduate Studies is being managed by a qualified, strict but warm and kind-hearted Dean. At present, there are three hundred eighteen (318) students enrolled in the program.
